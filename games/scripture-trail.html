SCRIPTURE TRAIL · HOW TO PLAY
A 30-minute trail through the lesson, together as two teams.
Lesson 23 · Ruth & 1 Samuel 1–7 · 7 stops · 3 story arcs
1
Pick teams and captains
Name your two teams, pick a captain for each, and choose a champion character. The captain on duty leads their team's turn.
2
Watch each story arc together
Before the first stop of each arc (Ruth, Hannah, Samuel), a 1–4 min Church video plays as the class watches together. It sets the context for the next 2–3 stops.
3
Take turns opening stops
Teams alternate. The captain on duty picks a teammate to answer the multiple-choice question. Read the verse together, or scan the QR to open it in Gospel Library.
4
Discuss as a class
After points are awarded, the discussion prompt opens it up to everyone — not just the team that answered. The Christ Connection ties the scripture to the Saviour.
↻
Go back if you need to
Completed stops stay clickable — open one to re-read the verse with the class, or to fix a score if a team was awarded by mistake.
📱 WHAT YOU'LL NEED
  • A screen the whole class can see — TV, projector, iPad, or large monitor
  • A phone with a QR scanner (any student's) to open verses in Gospel Library
  • About 30 minutes

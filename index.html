Build youth engagement into every lesson.

Research finds students in active-learning classrooms are 1.5× less likely to fall behind than peers in lecture-only ones. Kindred is built around the same idea — turn each lesson into something youth do, not something they sit through.

Source: Freeman et al., PNAS meta-analysis of 225 classroom studies, 2014

GAME 1 · COMMON GROUND

Survey-style game, scripture-centered.

Two teams. Four answers behind each card. Teens race to reveal what their peers said — then you pull the verse up on the Monitor and connect the answer back to Christ.

  • Monitor view on the classroom TV; admin view on your phone
  • Strikes, steals, switch-play — all the rhythm of the TV format
  • Every scripture-based round shows the source verse live on screen
  • Opening scripture + testimony invitation bookends every session

Memory matching with scripture depth.
